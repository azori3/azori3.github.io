I started Kickboxing at the age of 8 and immediately fell in love with the sport. I trained hard and competed in tournaments, eventually winning three Tunisian championships and two African championships (WKF - World Kickboxing Federation). With a professional fighting record of 17-4, I had the opportunity to fight around the world in locations such as Tunisia, Morocco, Algeria, Egypt, Nigeria, Ivory Coast, Lebanon, Turkey, Spain and France.


I started kickboxing to gain confidence and to learn self-defense. Over the years, I've seen firsthand how kickboxing can transform lives by improving fitness, building confidence, and teaching valuable self-defense skills.


That's why I'm committed to helping others achieve their fitness goals and learn how to protect themselves. Whether you're a beginner or a pro fighter, I can provide personalized training that's tailored to your needs and goals.
